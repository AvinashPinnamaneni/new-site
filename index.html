Back Chipping Machine for Circular Vessels

Field of application: Pressure vessel manufacturing

Backchipping is a machining process used in manufacturing to remove sharp edges or burrs from the backside of a workpiece. It involves creating a bevel or chamfer on the edge of a workpiece to smooth out rough edges or remove material for weld preparation.

Project tasks:
  • Selection of optimal tool and machining process for material removal.
  • Design of modular frame to accomodate easy adjustment of tool position and tool change.
